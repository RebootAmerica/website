Reboot America supports safe, prosperous communities





We find technologists local to the communities we work in.

We connect them with our partner advocacy organizations & policy think-tanks.

We build, prototype, and push to market public safety solutions with communities in need.

Our Programs

Through partnerships with local advocacy organizations, changemakers, companies and governments, Reboot America brings together experts from intersecting disciplines to power the future of co-created, community support





Reboots

Hackathons, unconferences, and workshops focusing on community engagement while building public safety solutions and prototypes with local changemakers and advocates

R & D

High-quality research conducted in partnership with universities, think-tanks and research groups that shapes our public safety proposals, solutions, and development

Public Education

Knowledge is power and we aim to share everything we know and learn

Community Partner

Hub at the intersection of advocacy, education, government and technology for people working towards equitable societies

How Will You Help?

Reboot America doesn't exist without our supporters and partners. The problems we're trying to solve are big, and together we can solve them.

Volunteer

Whether it's virtually or in-person, we're always looking for volunteers. Our biggest needs tend to be tech, research, and design, but we always need help with everything and anything you can imagine. Take a look at the form below, and if you don't see a match but still want to help, shoot us an email at hello@rebootamerica.tech

Volunteer

Contribute

We raise philanthropic capital from individual donors, foundations, and corporations who are interested in the intersection of tech and social good. We believe in ethical, corporate philanthropy and seek funders who share similar values.


Donate

Join Forces

We cannot do the work we do without our partners. We're interested in: policy think-tanks & research institutions focusing on any intersection of public safety; colleges & universities looking to provide employment opportunities to students; corporations, and more.
